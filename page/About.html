Connecting People in the Gaming Industry

About GSPHERE

GSPHERE is a dedicated space for developers, designers, and content creators to connect, collaborate, and build amazing games together.

Why GSPHERE?

  • Problem: In a booming $200B+ industry, talented creators struggle to find reliable partners and manage projects. Existing solutions (WhatsApp, Facebook, Discord) are not built for professional game development.
  • Our Solution: GSPHERE matches you with the right collaborators, provides professional profiles, and delivers integrated project management tools—all in one place.
  • Unique Value: Unlike one-off events and scattered communities, GSPHERE is your project “home base”—from partner recruitment to demo launches, feedback, and ongoing teamwork.

Our Vision

We aspire to empower the game creation community—connecting Israeli studios, indies, and creators—so anyone can easily join, collaborate, and succeed.

How We’re Different

While other communities focus on events and branding, GSPHERE is all about ongoing collaboration, project management, and daily opportunities.
You don’t just meet people—you build together, every day.
